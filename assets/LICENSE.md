# Artwork attribution

`gm-skull.svg` and `gm-skull.png` are adapted from
[Daemon skull](https://game-icons.net/1x1/lorc/daemon-skull.html) by
[Lorc](https://lorcblog.blogspot.com/), licensed under
[CC BY 3.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/).

The adaptation changes the original icon's colors to a pale lavender foreground
(`#e9d5ff`) on a dark purple background (`#210b35`). `gm-skull.png` is a
512-by-512 rasterization of the recolored SVG. No generative AI was used for
these adaptations.
