# Spotlight alert sounds

`spotlight-alert.flac` is `Braam` by unfa, downloaded from
[Wikimedia Commons](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:647712_unfa_braam.flac). The original was published on
[Freesound](https://freesound.org/people/unfa/sounds/647712/).

`player-claim-horn.mp3` is the unchanged published high-quality preview of
`tadaa.wav` by Maikkihapsis, downloaded from
[Freesound](https://freesound.org/people/Maikkihapsis/sounds/626950/). The sound
was released into the public domain under
[CC0 1.0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/).

`automatic-selection.mp3` is the unchanged published high-quality preview of
`String Wow` by akelley6, downloaded from
[Freesound](https://freesound.org/people/akelley6/sounds/825586/). The original
is licensed under [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

`gm-take-horn.mp3` is adapted from the published high-quality preview of
`Short scary violins.wav` by Victor_Natas, downloaded from
[Freesound](https://freesound.org/people/Victor_Natas/sounds/554754/). The
adaptation removes 1.137 seconds of leading silence and re-encodes the audio as
MP3; no other creative changes were made. The original is licensed under
[CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

`spotlight-alert.wav` is the legacy `bell_ding1.wav` alert by PWL, downloaded from
[OpenGameArt](https://opengameart.org/content/bell-dingschimes).

The other bundled sounds, except for the two explicitly attributed CC BY 4.0
cues above, were also released into the public domain under CC0 1.0.
